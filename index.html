Step-by-step instructions for performing Hajj correctly according to the Sunnah

Main Steps of Hajj

1 Ihram
Intention + Wear Ihram: Enter state of Ihram before Miqat. Men wear two white unstitched cloths.
2 Tawaf
7x Around Kaaba: Circumambulate Kaaba seven times counter-clockwise starting from Black Stone.
3 Day of Arafat
Most Important Day: Stand in prayer at Mount Arafat from noon until sunset on 9th Dhul-Hijjah.
4 Muzdalifah
Overnight Stay: Spend night in Muzdalifah after sunset. Collect pebbles for stoning ritual.
5 Rami (Stoning)
Stone Jamarat: Stone the largest Jamrah with 7 pebbles on 10th Dhul-Hijjah.
6 Qurbani
Animal Sacrifice: Offer sacrifice (sheep, goat, cow or camel) on 10th Dhul-Hijjah.
7 Halaq/Taqsir
Shave or Trim: Men shave head or trim hair. Women cut fingertip's length of hair.
8 Eid Celebrations
Eid al-Adha: Perform Eid prayers and celebrate after completing main rituals.

Final Steps & Completion

9 Tawaf al-Ifadah
Essential Tawaf: Perform Tawaf al-Ifadah after coming out of Ihram. This is a pillar of Hajj.
10 Rami Again
Continue Stoning: If staying in Mina for Tashreeq days, stone all three Jamarat each day after sunset.
11 Tawaf al-Wada
Farewell Tawaf: Final Tawaf before leaving Makkah. Obligatory for non-residents.
12 Completion
Hajj Mabrur: With sincere intention and proper completion, your Hajj is accepted (Insha'Allah).
